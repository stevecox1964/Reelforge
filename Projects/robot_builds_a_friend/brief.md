# Robot Builds A Friend

**Idea:** A lonely robot builds another robot to make a friend
**Type:** animated_story
**Duration:** 20 seconds
**Aspect ratio:** 16:9
**Voiceover:** yes

## Creative Direction

A tender, whimsical 3D animated short set in a cozy moonlit repair workshop. The main robot is small, brushed metal, warm amber eyes, careful hands, and a gentle expressive face. The friend robot is smaller, newly built, rounded, and has soft blue eyes. Keep the tone hopeful, family-friendly, cinematic, and emotionally readable.

## Scenes

- scene_001: The lonely robot notices it has no one to share the workshop with.
- scene_002: The robot carefully builds a second robot from spare parts.
- scene_003: The new robot wakes up for the first time.
- scene_004: The two robots awkwardly learn how to connect.
- scene_005: The robots share the morning together, no longer alone.

## Voiceover

Every night, one little robot fixed everything in the workshop, except its own lonely heart. So it gathered bolts, wires, and courage, and began building someone to say hello to. At last, a small blue light blinked back at it. They did not know how friendship worked, so they started with one tiny hand. By morning, the workshop was still full of spare parts, but now it was also full of friendship.
