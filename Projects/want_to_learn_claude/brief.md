# want_to_learn_claude

## Goal
A top-of-funnel awareness video that sparks curiosity about Claude, asks the audience what they want to learn, and drives comments, DMs, and website visits as lead capture.

## Video Type
explainer

## Audience
Everyday people — business owners, entrepreneurs, team leads, side hustlers — who have heard of AI but don't know what Claude is or why it matters for them. Not coders or marketers.

## Format
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Target duration: 20 seconds
- Style: Talking head + B-roll hybrid — direct-to-camera energy with bold graphic text overlays, clean modern social media aesthetic
- Voiceover: yes

## Concept
This is NOT a tutorial. It is a curiosity hook designed to make the audience raise their hand. The video opens with "Do you want to learn Claude Code?", exposes the gap in accessible content, asks what the viewer wants to learn, and ends with a dual CTA: comment YES, DM the creator, and visit hephosnexa.com.

## Scene Flow
1. Hook — "Do you want to learn Claude Code?"
2. The gap — 99% of content is for coders, not you
3. The question — "What do YOU want to learn about Claude?"
4. CTA — "Comment YES, DM me, or go to hephosnexa.com"

## Constraints
- Must include: bold text overlays as visual emphasis for each key question
- Must include: dual CTA (comment YES + DM me) and URL hephosnexa.com in final scene
- Must avoid: tutorial content, how-to steps, code, technical jargon
- Must avoid: any mention of repurposing or training business strategies
- Platform targets: Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn

## Approval Points
- Approve scene plan before paid generation
- Approve still frames before image-to-video
- Approve voiceover script before TTS
- Approve final clips before assembly
